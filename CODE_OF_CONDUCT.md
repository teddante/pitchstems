# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want PitchStems to be a practical and welcoming project for people working
with audio, music, and local AI tools.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections gracefully.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.
- Do not harass, threaten, or discriminate against others.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when
behavior makes the project unsafe or unproductive.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer once the repository is
published.
